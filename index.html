📌 Project Overview

This is not a history handout — it is a civilizational counterstrike.

Born from moral outrage against NCERT’s glorification of invaders, this project exposes the brutality of the Mughal Empire: temple demolitions, forced conversions, and erasure of Brahminical knowledge.

Backed by native chronicles and eyewitness accounts, this is a call to break curated amnesia and reclaim Bharat’s civilizational memory.
